Who are we?

Koalateer is a nonprofit organization where we match students with volunteer opportunities that cater to their interests. This is to limit uncertainty through third-party websites and to get reliable information on an opportunity with interest to the individual. Our application plans to have a rewarding aspect to it, to make volunteering competitive, yet fun, and a social media aspect for all volunteers to connect and share their experiences.

This company was inspired by students who themselves had struggled to find volunteer positions, especially during quarantine, to fulfill requirements and for the college admissions process. This took the fun out of volunteering. With Koalateer we are shifting the mindset of volunteering from an obligation to a joyful, exciting activity.

A few statistics

High school and college students today are less likely to volunteer or give to charity than those of 15 years ago. A lack of awareness of opportunities and the inabilty to find volunteer opportunities that lead them to stop volunteering all together.

Only 55% of nonprofits assess volunteer impact, Koalateer does.

Over the past decade, teenage volunteer rates haved declined by over 5%.

Less than a quarter donate to charaity, a rate that has been flat since 2008.

Koalateer is focused on increasing the rate of student volunteers by providing a source of available opportunities that align with their specific interests and abilities.
